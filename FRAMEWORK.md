# Cosmic Reach Creative — Site Framework Reference

---

## Signal · Messaging & Brand Clarity
The right words, for the right people, in the right order.
- **Weak:** Visitors can't explain what you do after 5 seconds
- **Weak:** Copy describes features instead of outcomes
- **Weak:** Voice shifts inconsistently across pages
- **Works:** Ideal clients self-identify immediately
- **Works:** Headlines create curiosity and pull readers forward
- **Works:** Brand voice feels distinct and trustworthy

---

## Gravity · Offer Strength & Conversion Logic
A compelling offer with a clear path from interest to action.
- **Weak:** CTAs are vague ("Learn More") or absent
- **Weak:** Value proposition isn't tied to a specific result
- **Weak:** Pricing or next steps feel ambiguous
- **Works:** Visitors know exactly what to do next
- **Works:** Offer feels low-risk and high-reward
- **Works:** Conversion points are natural, not forced

---

## Orbit · Marketing Systems & Workflow
The repeatable systems that keep leads moving without friction.
- **Weak:** No consistent content or follow-up cadence
- **Weak:** Tools and channels aren't connected
- **Weak:** Audience growth depends on manual effort
- **Works:** Nurture runs automatically after first touch
- **Works:** Content compounds over time
- **Works:** Team has a clear, repeatable playbook

---

## Thrust · KPI Visibility & Performance Insight
Real data that shows what's working and what to fix next.
- **Weak:** No baseline metrics to measure against
- **Weak:** Decisions are made on gut feeling, not evidence
- **Weak:** Traffic and conversions aren't tied to revenue
- **Works:** Weekly dashboard shows top-line performance
- **Works:** Bottlenecks are spotted before they cost money
- **Works:** Every edit is informed by a specific metric
