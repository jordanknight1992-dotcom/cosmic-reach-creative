## Strategic Assessment

### Is the Clarity Audit the right marketing engine?

**Yes, and here's why it's your strongest asset right now:**

1. **Low-friction entry point.** $150 is impulse-level for a business owner who feels stuck. No discovery call required, no proposal phase, no negotiation.
2. **Delivers disproportionate value.** A full scored diagnostic report with implementation roadmap is something agencies charge $2K+ for. The price-to-value gap generates word-of-mouth.
3. **Built-in upsell path.** The audit naturally reveals the work that needs doing, which maps directly to your Sprint tiers. You're not selling; you're diagnosing, then offering treatment.
4. **Creates shareable proof.** Each completed audit gives you anonymized data points, frameworks, and case patterns you can turn into content.

**The play is simple:** Get the audit into as many hands as possible. The report sells the Sprints. The Sprints sell Mission Control.

## Appendix A: Adobe Firefly Prompts

### Brand Visual Guidelines

Before generating, set these parameters in Adobe Firefly:

**Consistent Settings for ALL prompts:**
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:5 (LinkedIn optimal for feed posts) or 16:9 (for newsletter headers)
- **Content Type:** Art
- **Style:** Flat, minimalist, clean digital illustration
- **Color & Tone → Reference Colors:** Use these hex codes as guidance for overall palette:
  - Primary dark: `#0b1120` (deep navy/space)
  - Accent warm: `#d4a574` (warm copper/gold)
  - Light neutral: `#e8dfcf` (warm off-white/cream)
  - Dark blue: `#111827` (dark blue-gray)
- **Lighting:** Cinematic, moody, subtle warm highlights
- **Camera Angle:** Straight-on or slight top-down for abstract compositions
- **Negative Prompt / Avoid:** Cartoonish, neon colors, busy backgrounds, text, faces, clip art, stock photo style
