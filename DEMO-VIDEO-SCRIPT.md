# Cosmic Reach Creative — Demo Video Script

**Total runtime target:** 4–5 minutes
**Tone:** Confident, unhurried, founder-talking-to-a-peer. Not salesy.
**Recording tool:** Loom or QuickTime (screen + mic)
**Resolution:** 1920x1080, browser at ~90% zoom so UI reads clearly

---

## PRE-RECORDING CHECKLIST

Before you hit record:

- [ ] Open cosmicreachcreative.com in Chrome (incognito for clean state)
- [ ] Open admin dashboard in a second tab (already logged in, 2FA done)
- [ ] In admin: make sure Mission Control has at least 2-3 real leads in different pipeline stages
- [ ] In admin: have one email draft saved (partially written) on a lead
- [ ] Close all other tabs, silence notifications
- [ ] Browser bookmarks bar hidden (Cmd+Shift+B)

---

## SCENE 1 — THE HOOK (0:00–0:20)

**[Screen: Homepage hero, slowly scrolling]**

> "Most creative agencies have a website, a calendar link, and a spreadsheet full of leads they never follow up on. I built a system that connects all three — and I want to show you how it works."

**Action:** Sit on the homepage hero for 3 seconds, then begin a slow scroll.

---

## SCENE 2 — THE PUBLIC SITE (0:20–1:15)

**[Screen: Continue scrolling homepage]**

> "This is Cosmic Reach Creative — my consultancy site. It's built on Next.js 15 and deployed on Vercel. Everything you see is custom — no templates, no WordPress."

**Action:** Scroll through the four-force framework section (Signal, Gravity, Orbit, Thrust).

> "The site itself is the pitch. It walks prospects through my framework, shows pricing, and funnels them toward one action—"

**Action:** Click "Book a Call" in the header nav.

> "—booking a call."

---

## SCENE 3 — THE BOOKING FLOW (1:15–2:15)

**[Screen: /connect page — session type selection]**

> "Clients pick a session type. Free signal checks, paid clarity audits, or open connect calls at 30, 60, or 90 minutes."

**Action:** Click "Connect with Jordan — 30 min" (or whichever you prefer to demo).

**[Screen: /book/connect-30 — calendar view]**

> "They see real-time availability pulled from my Google Calendar. No double-bookings, no back-and-forth emails. Pick a day, pick a time."

**Action:** Click a date with available slots. Click a time slot.

> "Name, email, optional notes — that's it."

**Action:** Don't actually submit (you don't want a fake booking). Instead, pause here.

> "When they confirm, three things happen automatically: a Google Calendar event is created with a Meet link, the client gets a branded confirmation email with an .ics attachment, and I get an alert. For paid sessions like the Business Clarity Audit, the confirmation includes a Stripe payment link — they can't skip it."

**Action:** Cut to a screenshot of the confirmation email (prepare this beforehand from a real booking). Show it for 4-5 seconds.

---

## SCENE 4 — MISSION CONTROL / CRM (2:15–3:30)

**[Screen: Switch to admin tab — Mission Control]**

> "Here's where it gets interesting. Every booking automatically creates a lead in my CRM — I call it Mission Control."

**Action:** Show the pipeline KPI bar at the top (lead counts by stage).

> "I can see every lead, what stage they're in, when I last contacted them, and what's next."

**Action:** Click on a lead to open the detail panel.

> "Each lead has a full profile — company, contact info, notes, activity history. I can move them through pipeline stages with one click."

**Action:** Click a pipeline stage button to advance the lead (e.g., "Meeting Booked" → "Proposal Sent").

> "And when it's time to reach out — I write the email right here."

**Action:** Click into the email draft section. Show an existing draft or click "Generate with AI."

> "I can write it myself or let AI generate a first draft based on the lead's context. Edit it, preview it in a branded template, and send — all without leaving the dashboard."

**Action:** Show the branded email preview for 3-4 seconds.

---

## SCENE 5 — PROSPECTING WITH APOLLO (3:30–4:00)

**[Screen: Still in Mission Control — click Apollo Search]**

> "I also prospect directly from here. Apollo integration lets me search by industry, title, and location — find the right person, and import them as a lead in one click."

**Action:** Type a search (e.g., "Marketing Director" in "Memphis"). Show results. Click "Import" on one.

> "Rate-limited so I don't burn through the API, and I can suppress companies I don't want to see again."

---

## SCENE 6 — ANALYTICS (4:00–4:20)

**[Screen: Click "Digital Signals" in sidebar]**

> "Digital Signals pulls live data from Google Analytics — sessions, top pages, traffic sources. I can see what's working without opening another tool."

**Action:** Show the GA4 dashboard for 3-4 seconds. Don't linger.

---

## SCENE 7 — THE CLOSE (4:20–4:45)

**[Screen: Back to Mission Control, or homepage]**

> "Booking, CRM, email, analytics, payments — one system, one login, two-factor secured. I built this for myself, but the architecture works for any service business that runs on relationships."

> "If you want something like this for your agency — that's literally what I do. Book a signal check, link's in the description."

**[Screen: Hold on homepage hero or /connect page for 3 seconds, then end.]**

## POST-PRODUCTION NOTES

**Editing (Descript recommended):**
- Cut any pauses longer than 1.5 seconds
- Add subtle zoom-ins when clicking important UI elements (Descript has this built in)
- Add a lower-third title card at 0:00: "Cosmic Reach Creative — Full Platform Demo"
- Add a lower-third at 4:20: "cosmicreachcreative.com/connect"
- Background music: low, ambient, electronic. Epidemic Sound or Artlist — search "minimal tech" or "SaaS demo"

**Thumbnail:**
- Split screen: left = the public site hero, right = Mission Control dashboard
- Text overlay: "I Built My Own CRM"
- Your face in bottom corner if posting to YouTube

**Export:** 1080p, H.264, upload to YouTube (unlisted or public) and embed on site if desired.

---

## DESCRIPT WORKFLOW

1. Record full screen + mic in Loom or QuickTime (~8-10 min raw, don't rush)
2. Import into Descript — it auto-transcribes
3. Delete filler words, long pauses, mistakes by deleting text (video follows)
4. Use "Studio Sound" to clean up audio
5. Add Scene headings as markers
6. Export final cut

## ELEVENLABS VOICEOVER ALTERNATIVE

If you'd rather not use your own voice:
1. Go to elevenlabs.io → Voice Cloning → upload a 30-60 second clip of yourself talking
2. Paste each scene's narration into the text box
3. Generate, download each clip
4. Drop into Descript timeline aligned to the screen recording

**I recommend your real voice.** It's a trust play — you're selling yourself.
